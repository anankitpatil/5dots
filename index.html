Why 5D Life?

Ever wondered why your current fitness routines aren’t as effective? Well, that’s because they haven’t been designed for you.

All your health trackers, diet plans, weight loss programs, nutraceuticals etc. are either expensive, inaccessible or give way too much data than you need.

Their effects might be visible in the short run, but their methods aren’t neither sustainable nor do they focus on the most critical aspect of health care. Heart Rate. That’s what we’re out to solve.

The globally acclaimed HeartZones® USA powering your programs.

The heart rate zone theory has been designed by Sally Edwards (5D expert) which over the years has become a standard in global fitness. By ensuring your training revolves around what is considered to be the ‘ideal heart rate’, this breakthrough theory allows your routines to be a lot of more effective and aim at strengthening your heart muscles.

The cornerstone of 5D Life is built on the proven and patented concept of heart zones and fitness training. We strongly believe that nutrition and exercise go hand in hand for long lasting results and your efforts must improve your HEART HEALTH. That’s the only score that matters for us... and you.

Seamless Integration with any device. Don’t have one yet? No worries.

At 5D Life it’s all about you. The app allows you to keep track of your daily activities by capturing all your data through apps, wearable devices or even smartphone sensors. Seamless integration across multiple devices you may own, aggregating all your data in one account. More so over, all this data, once collected, is stored on our cloud platform for eternity.

And for those who don’t own a wearable device, you can either use our in-app heart rate monitor to capture all the data your coach needs to create customized training programs.
